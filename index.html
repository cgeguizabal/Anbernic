Retro square-screen design

Retro square-screen, Linux 64-bit system and equipped with RGB lighting effects to bring you a cool gaming experience!

Anbernic RG CubeXX is a high-cost-performance retro square-screen handheld console, featuring a 3.95-inch IPS fully laminated display with a resolution of 720x720. Its 1:1 aspect ratio ensures excellent compatibility with various emulators, including vertical arcade games and other 30+ gaming platforms. The overall design of the device is elegant, with a reasonable button layout and comfortable grip and feel.
